# Status Report: MLS Player Performance and Salary Analysis
## Overview
Our project analyzes whether player salary in Major League Soccer relects measureable on field performance. We are integrating two public datasets: 2025 MLS player statistics from FBref and player compensation data from the MLS Players Association Salary Guide. The goal is to determine whether high paid players tend to produce stronger statistical outputs, identify which performance variables are most associated with compensation, and explore whether some players appear overpaid or underpaid relative to production.
